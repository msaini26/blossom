Businesses we Bloomed

Cafe con Libros

A hybrid coffee shop and intersectional Feminist bookstore in Prospect Heights, Brooklyn, NY that opened in 2017, this welcoming neighborhood spot hosts monthly book club meet-ups for women of color.

Cooks Nook

Cooks Nook is an independent retail store in Wilton CT, serving people who love to cook in the Tri-State Area since 1998. Their customers say, "If you can't find it here, you won't find it anywhere".

Womancraft Gifts

A unique shopping experience in Carrboro, NC. As a cop-op, the artists create and sell their work as well as manage the business operations and they are on hand to share their stories.

The Potted Edge

A plant lovers delight, offering one-of-a-kind succulent arrangements, thoughtfully curated interior plants and a unique selection of handmade items and pottery located in Gallipolis, OH.

Roosters Mens Grooming Center

The Roosters experience, located in Irvine CA, combines modern grooming techniques with classic barbershop elements. From the moment our clients arrive, it's as though they have stepped back in time to a simpler way of life.

Inkwood Books

Shop all the best books, in-store or online in Haddonfield, NJ. They've got an incredible selection, knowledgeable staff, a virtual book fair, and plenty more to enjoy. Give the gift of locally-bought literature and make the bibliophile in your life jump for joy!
